Our Story

History & Capabilities

Founded in 2010, SealTech Industrial began with a single vision: to eliminate the packaging bottleneck for hardware manufacturers. What started as a small manual sorting facility has grown into a state-of-the-art packaging operation serving the entire Midwest.

Mission

To provide unparalleled reliability and accuracy in hardware packaging, ensuring our clients' supply chains run smoother.

Vision

To be the trusted extension of every OEM assembly line, known for zero-defect packaging and transparent communication.

Values

Integrity, Precision, and Partnership. We treat your components with the same care as if they were our own.
